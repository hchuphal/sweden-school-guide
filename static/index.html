Expat guide

How Swedish school choice works

F0 means förskoleklass

Förskoleklass is the transition year before grade 1. Schools often list it as “F” in F–6 or F–9.

Municipal schools

Göteborg placement uses municipal rules such as sibling priority, relative proximity, absolute proximity and lottery where needed.

Fristående schools

Independent schools are applied to directly. Queue date, sibling priority and sometimes own preschool priority can matter more than distance.

Use official sources

The app is a screening tool. Always verify with Göteborg Stad, Skolverket/Utbildningsguiden and each school before deciding.
